# Escalation Policy
Version: 1.3
Effective date: 2025-09-10
Status: Active

Escalate immediately when:
- confidence score is below threshold
- documents conflict and no active policy clearly overrides
- the request involves regulated cargo
- the request involves temperature-sensitive cargo
- the customer requests legal or contractual interpretation

Human escalation targets:
- Pricing operations: pricing disputes
- Operations lead: shipment exceptions
- Compliance: regulated or legal-sensitive requests
